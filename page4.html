PHYSICAL HEALTH

Physical health is the ability of a person to function effectively in fulfilling daily activities and use their free time effectively. Individuals who achieve physical health have the endurance of any disease that can affect the function of the body. Their body organs can function properly. Individuals who achieve physical health also have optimum fitness levels and have the ability to do daily activities effectively. His body’s senses have strengthness to work well. Physical health is generally characterized by physical activeness either energetic body or otherwise.

An overview from physical’s doctor about physical health.

Physical health also affects mental health. There also have strongly connected between physical health and mental health. Physical health is a condition being free from illness or injury. Physical health encourages us to take care for our bodies through healthy self-care, physical activity and proper diet and nutrition.

Physical health important took into consideration about taking alcohol, drug and smoking that can cause health problem and bad risks that can effect physical health if the intake of alcohol, drug and smoking is excessive.

Medical self-care should be concerned if you had long term illness such as cough, fever, stomach ache or any other illness. Emergency treatment must be done when signs and symptoms getting worse or life-threatening.

Allowing the body to rest enough also important. Sleep at night day should take place in a calm, quiet and comfortable environment and should last about 7-9 hours. Spending time relaxing or taking some short naps also can help refresh the body for a long day. In Islamic perspectives, the best time for taking some short naps that called at Qailullah time that mean a short nap for a few minutes before entering Zuhur or after lunch until early Asar with a time between 10 to 30 minutes. The most recent time is within half an hour before the time of Zuhur prayers.

The healthiest children and adults need to be active every day. Being physically active with various physical activities is important to keeping your body in its top condition. This should be a combination of both physical activity and structured exercise such as jogging, biking, strength training and sports.

A well-balanced diet should contain minerals, fats, calcium, carbohydrates, proteins and vitamins. Important to nurture our body with get it through supervision of a licensed health professional to perform by eating a well-balanced diet. Meals and snacks can also be taken but in portion sizes that should be convenient and also regularly consumed a mineral water.

Physical health is of paramount importance to the health of everybody. Various diseases can occur as a cause of not maintaining a good health and lack of physical health care. Keeping your physical health manifestly scientifically improves mental well-being and conversely. If one from that is ignored, the other can also be affected.

Interview : Student

Question 1 : What are the exercising on budget for student?

There are a lots of way on exercise to keep fit without spending the money moreover for university students. For example, join a sport club, fitness apps, find an outdoor gym and the others. In addition, it is necessary to take into consideration the preparedness before doing the physical activities. But before making the exercise, everyone should know what type of physical activity is suitable for them. For example, people with health problems cannot perform the same type of physical activity as professional sportsmen that it will give the bad impact to them.

1. Join a sport club

Being on a budget, basically each of university will offer a number of sport clubs such as football, table tennis, badminton or anything else. So, just take the advantages to join this club. Alternatively, you may even want to start up your own sports team by a group members.

2. Fitness applications

There are some great apps there if you have a smartphone, for little or no cost at all and the best part is that loads of them are free. So, just select the best apps for you to workout plan.

3. Create own home gym 

For a small cost, you don’t need a fancy gym to do bodyweight exercises. Just find some suitable heavy objects like use the full plastic bottles with filled with rice or something heavy. It is very simple things to buy because it is not too expensive and also easy to keep store. At the same time, you can still exercise at home by running up and down the stairs to get your heart going. Everything comes from yourself.

4. Walk

Building up stamina over time is must, so the first thing you should do is replace your trips on public transport with walking. Why you not get off the bus one step early before class? It will helps you calculate calories burned during exercise, even walking.


Question 2 : When is the best time of day to work out?

Actually, the truth is its personal things. We all knows that some people might busy with their work hard to do anything else. It depends on someone when is the best time, because the benefits of physical activity depend upon how consistent you are. Maybe dawn or maybe early in the morning. For example, of you are not a morning person, it may not work for you and try to get up at dawn to work out. Plenty of people look the benefits of early morning to work out, but if you cannot fit in a workout before noon, don’t sweat it. It will nothing for your health. But, there are some tips here that “exercise anytime is better than none at all” said Mohammad Saufi Bin Mohamad Fadzli, come FKP’s student.


Question 3 : How to make exercise be more fun?

1. Exercise with a friend

Exercise with a friend or loved one can make it more fun. We are less likely to get bored when have a work out buddy, especially a friend. By going with a friend, we can get more motivation from them and also can laugh together in the process of exercising.

2. Choose an exercise love 

Select an exercise that we love are something funny. For instance, if we love dancing, then take up dance classes. That is the simplest way we can do to make an exercise more fun and good.

3. Listen to music 

With so many podcast and music apps available on our smartphone, we can easily find entertainment options perfectly suite to our personal tastes. Never worry what people think about us, just use an earphones and get lost in our own world. As we all know, music can improve a good performance by listening to a beat of music.
